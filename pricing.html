Pricing FAQ

print pricing + shirt pricing = total cost

    Print Pricing

    The cost of ink is determined by the size, not colors, of the design.

    $4.00 - Pocket or a 4" tall design

    $8.00 - 8" tall design

    $12.00 - 12" tall design

    $16.00 - 16" tall design

    *Ink Prices are subject to change

    Shirt Pricing

    Short Sleeve Tees: $8 - 10

    Long Sleeve Tees: $12

    Youth Tees: $4 - 7

    Crewnecks: $15

    Hoodies: $22

    Call or email to inquire about additional shirt prices.

    Discounts

    10 - 19 Items = 10% off total

    20 - 49 Items = 20% off total

    50+ Items = 30% off total

    Full Time BYUI Faculty = 10% off total

    *No Special IBC Discounts

    *One discount per order

    Bringing In Your Own Shirt

    You are more than welcome to bring in your own article of clothing. But...

    • There is a $7 bring in fee on top of the cost of ink

    • The item must be 100% cotton or a 60/40 cotton poly blend

    • We cannot 100% guarantee any outside material brought in will work with our ink or printers

    • There are no replacements or reimbursements for shirts brought in
